Welcome to the British Wildlife Centre

The British Wildlife Centre is dedicated to the conservation and protection of native UK wildlife. We provide a safe haven for animals in need while educating visitors about the importance of preserving our natural heritage.

From the iconic red fox to the endangered water vole, Britain is home to an incredible diversity of wildlife. Many of these species face challenges from habitat loss, climate change, and human activity. Our mission is to protect these animals and their habitats for future generations.
